==========================================
    EASY MART INVENTORY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
==========================================

QUICK START:
------------
1. Double-click "START_EASYMART.bat" to launch the application
2. The app will open in your default web browser

LOGIN CREDENTIALS:
------------------
Manager:  manager123
Cashier:  cashier123
Customer: customer123

FEATURES:
---------
- Add, edit, and delete products (Manager)
- Process sales with barcode scanning (Cashier)
- View stock status in real-time (Customer)
- All data is saved automatically and persists after closing

DATA PERSISTENCE:
-----------------
All your data (products, sales, stock levels) is automatically 
saved in your browser's local storage. Your data will remain 
even after closing the browser or restarting the computer.

MULTI-WINDOW MODE:
------------------
Visit /launcher in the URL to open all three interfaces 
in separate windows for multi-display setups.

TIPS:
-----
- Use the "Add Product" button to add new inventory items
- Scan barcodes using your webcam or enter them manually
- Stock updates sync across all open windows in real-time
